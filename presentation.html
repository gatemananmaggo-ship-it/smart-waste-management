The Problem

Inefficient Collection: Trucks following fixed routes even for empty bins.

Carbon Footprint: Excessive fuel consumption and vehicle wear.

Health Hazards: Overflowing bins leading to disease and foul odor.

"Our cities are growing, but our waste management is stuck in the past."

Our Solution: EcoSmart

A closed-loop IoT ecosystem that turns real-time data into efficient action.

Smart Bins

ESP8266 & Ultrasonic sensors reporting live levels.

Real-time Cloud

MERN backend with instant Socket.io updates.

Route Optimization

AI-driven paths for collection workers via OSRM.

Predictive Trends

Identifying high-volume areas before they overflow.

Live Monitoring Dashboard

  • Visual Map of all bins across the city.
  • Dynamic Color Coding (Green/Orange/Red).
  • Battery Health & Connectivity Alerts.
  • Historical Analytics to spot peak usage periods.
